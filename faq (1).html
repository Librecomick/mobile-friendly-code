These terms refer to comics from different regions:

  • Manga: Japanese comics, traditionally read from right to left
  • Manhwa: Korean comics, typically read from left to right or in a vertical scroll format
  • Manhua: Chinese comics, read from left to right
  • Webtoon: Digital comics optimized for vertical scrolling, popular in Korea

New chapters and series are added daily. The frequency depends on scanlation groups and official releases. You can check the "Recently Added" section on the homepage or enable notifications for your followed comics to stay updated.

Content & Updates

Missing chapters can occur for several reasons:

  • The scanlation group hasn't released them yet
  • Chapters were removed due to copyright claims
  • Technical issues during upload
  • The series is licensed and removed per publisher request

Check the comic's details page for information about chapter availability.

While we don't accept direct requests, we constantly monitor popular series and work with scanlation groups to expand our library. If a series gains enough popularity or official translations become available, we'll add it to our platform.

This filter on the homepage shows only chapters that have been translated into English. When enabled, you won't see chapters in other languages. This helps you quickly find readable content in your preferred language.

If you encounter inappropriate content, use the "Report" button on the comic's page or chapter. Provide detailed information about the issue, and our moderation team will review it within 24-48 hours.

Technical Issues

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Check your internet connection

If the problem persists, the image server might be experiencing high traffic. Try again in a few minutes.

Unicomick works best on modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (macOS and iOS)
  • Opera

Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version for the best experience.
